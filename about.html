About

My name is Ben Stone and this is my multimedia site.

I created this site as a requirement for Unit 1/2 Interactive Digital Media. The website contains all my projects over the past year (2016). The projects were completed with the extensive use of Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Flash.
